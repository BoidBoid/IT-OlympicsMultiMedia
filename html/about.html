Diversity In Technology

When people first hear the word “minority”, they most likely would think of different races. But there is more to the word “minority”. It could be included from different genders, learning disabilities, and religious groups. We want to give opportunities to all minorites, so they can have the freedom as everyone else does. Whether we are talking about pay checks, job opportunities, or first impressons, they all have something in common. Minorities are at a disadvantage. Its time to change the view of minorities, and to give them a free environment where they feel welcome to all.
